Closed Captions
 

DScaler has basic support for Closed Captions.

Closed Captions exist on:

  1. TV broadcasts in countries with 60Hz TV signals
  2. Video Tapes with the Q symbol
  3. Laserdiscs
  4. Some DVDs

To enable closed captions first turn on VBI capture by ensuriing the "Datacasting"/"Capture VBI" menu item is ticked. Then selecting the CC type to be displayed from the "Datacasting"/"Closed Captions" list. Normally you will want CC 1.

To switch closed captions off select "CC Off" from the "Datacasting"/"Closed Captions" menu.
