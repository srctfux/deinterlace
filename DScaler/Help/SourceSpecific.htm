Source Specific Settings
 

Many of the settings that DScaler uses for individual sources are configurable.

Whilst most users should use the default setting we know there are people out there who will want to tweak DScaler.

All the settings in DScaler have been optimized to run with many sources. However you may find that certain settings work better with your setup.

If you feel that your settings are better than the defaults over a wide range of material please let us know.
