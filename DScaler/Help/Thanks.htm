Thanks 
 

The DScaler team would like to express their gratitude to:

  • All the authors who have contributed code to the project.
  • Expresso for creating multidec the foundation of DScaler. MultiDec's thanks.
  • Mathias Ellinger for the driver from FreeTV on which DScaler's driver is based.
  • Daniel Schmelzer for his hard work creating and updating the website. 
  • Yvon Quere for creating and updating the FAQ.
  • Yvon and Ali for finding, using and promoting bTV and encouraging us to see HTPC's as video processors.
  • Jason Castle for updating the todo and bugs pages.
  • Borg, creator of bTV, for writing the first ever intelligent computer deinterlacing program--the inspiration for this project.
  • All at the AVSForums for your encouragement and help. The HTPC forum is easily the best group on the internet.
  • The authors of VirtualDub for the the idea behind the PAL detection algorithm.
  • Gunnar Thalin for his area based deinterlacing plug-in which was the basis for the Video Bob deinterlacing method.
  • Steaphan Greene, Øyvind Aabling, Octavian Purdila, Vladimir Dergachev and Christian Lupien—the GATOS developers—for the original screen capture code.
  • Jim Ferguson for his ground breaking YXY tool, the model and benchmark for aspect ratio support.
  • Gerd Knorr for the v4l2 driver for saa7134 on which DScaler's saa713x support is based and for providing details on the saa7134 chip.
  • Erik Språng for the new DScaler logo.
  • Phat Phreddy for the new DScaler domains.
  • Tom Fojta for the Czech teletext code page.
  • John Isar for the Greek teletext code page.
  • Eduardo José Tagle for the PAL-NC patch.
  • Robin Zalek (aka Bob) for the new icon.
