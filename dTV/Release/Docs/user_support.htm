User Support  
 

Currently, all support for DScaler is provided through the AVS Home Theater PC Forum, the largest such forum on the internet and a great place to pick up information and advice. Please feel free to stop by and say hello. You can post questions, bug reports, and suggestions there. We suggest that you do a search of the forum to ensure that your question has not already been answered.
