Passion of Mind DScaler v. WinDVD 

Passion of Mind (DVD)

Below is an A/B comparison provided by Li On of Passion of Mind with WinDVD and DScaler (dTV version 1.6 Beta 4). We aren't going to tell you which is which. On this picture, the two are almost indistinguishable.

Title: Passion of Mind
Sources: WinDVD v. Sony S7000 DVD player (for DScaler)
Connection: S-Video (for DScaler)
Capture/Tuner Card: Zoltrix TV-Max (for DScaler)
GeForce MX Overlay Settings: Brightness 130, Contrast 95, Hue default and Saturation 95 using reference 6.18 driver.
DScaler Picture Control Settings: Brightness 18, Contrast 205, Color 216, U/V at default and overscan at 2%
