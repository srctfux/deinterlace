Super Speedway DScaler v. WinDVD 

Super Speedway (DVD)

Below is an A/B comparison provided by Li On of Super Speedway with WinDVD and DScaler (dTV version 1.6 Beta 4). Click on the image below that you think is DScaler. On this shot, the two are almost indistinguishable.

Title: Super Speedway
Sources: WinDVD v. Sony S7000 DVD player (for DScaler)
Connection: S-Video (for DScaler)
Capture/Tuner Card: Zoltrix TV-Max (for DScaler)
GeForce MX Overlay Settings: Brightness 130, Contrast 95, Hue default and Saturation 95 using reference 6.18 driver.
