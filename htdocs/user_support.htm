User Support  
 

Currently, all support for dTV is provided through the AVS Home Theater PC Forum, the largest such forum on the internet and a great place to pick up information and advice. You can post questions, bug reports, and suggestions there. We suggest that you do a search of the forum to ensure that your item is not duplicative.  When using this search you must select "Home Theater Computers" in the Search Forum/Archive box.

Please do not email the developers directly about dTV.

Support is given by the helpful members of the community, whether they be involved in this project or not. However, some people that may appear often to answer user questions and who are affiliated with project include:
