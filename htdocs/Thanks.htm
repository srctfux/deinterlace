Thanks 
 

See Authors for up to date list of developers who have contributed code to the project.

See MultiDec_ReadMe.txt for a list of thanks for the project dTV was based upon.

  1. A big debt a gratitude is due to Mark Rejhon for his 3:2 algorithm and his help testing, promoting dTV but particularly for his vision and his refusal to accept second best.
  2. Thanks to Paul Samblanet, who contributed the aspect ratio control code.
  3. Thanks to Daniel Schmelzer for his hard work creating and updating the website. Love the logo! 
  4. Thanks to Steve Grimm for his work on the old method of 3:2 pulldown and to his contribution of the aspect ratio autodetect code.
  5. Thanks to Tom Barry for his optimization of the assembly code and his contribution of low-level user manipulation of the bt chipset.
  6. Thanks to Yvon Quere and Ali for finding bTV and Yvon for creating and updating the FAQ.
  7. Thanks to Jason Castle for updating the todo and bugs pages.
  8. Thanks to everybody on the deinterlace project mailing list.
  9. Thanks to Borg, creator of bTV, for writing the first ever computer deinterlacing program--the inspiration for this project.
  10. Thanks to all at the AVSForums for your encouragement and help. The HTPC forum is easily the best group on the internet.
  11. Thanks to the authors of VirtualDub for the the PAL detection algorithm.
  12. Thanks to Gunnar Thalin for his area based deinterlacing plug-in which was the basis for the current deinterlacing method.
  13. Thanks to Steaphan Greene, Øyvind Aabling, Octavian Purdila, Vladimir Dergachev and Christian Lupien—the GATOS developers—for the screen capture code.
