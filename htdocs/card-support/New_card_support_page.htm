Cards Supported 
 

DScaler 4 supports capture cards in two ways: (1) Native support, whereby DScaler takes direct control of the card to perform needed functions; and (2) WDM driver support, whereby DScaler relies on the card manufacturer's WDM driver to perform needed functions. Below is a list of capture cards that are natively supported by DScaler 4, in alphabetical order. If your card is not natively supported by DScaler 4, you may still be able to use DScaler's WDM driver support, as described above.
