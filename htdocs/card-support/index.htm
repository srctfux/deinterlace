Cards Supported 

Here is a list of the cards that should be supported by DScaler (list taken from the linux bttv driver on which DScaler was originally based--subsequently modified based on user reports). Those cards that have been confirmed by users as working are noted as such.

NOTE: The capture/tuner portion of all video card/capture card combos are not supported at this time. These cards include ATI-based boards, such as the ATI All-In Wonder, and GeForce cards, such as the Elsa Gladiac VIVO line. The video card portion of these cards works with DScaler, however.
