Various HD Screenshots 

HDTV (1080i)

Below are various HD screenshots provided by Li On. These screenshots are of 1080i video downconverted to 480i and fed into dTV version 2.0.2 using s-video. Of course, it would be preferrable in most normal viewing conditions to view the 1080i video directly without DScaler, but these screenshots show the versatility of DScaler in select circumstances.

Please note that these are 720 x 480 overlay grabs and that they would be squeezed by DScaler for normal viewing conditions to the 16:9 aspect ratio.
