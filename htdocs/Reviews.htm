dTV Reviews 
 

Mark Rejhon reviews v1.2 and comments on the newly supported video-source deinterlacing...

"Fast motion sports such as Football, Hockey and downhill skiing TV shows look MUCH better now with the new dTV 1.2 video-source deinterlacer. Anything with fast panning motion is very noticeably improved. No more venetian-blinds effects!"

CH Yeow comments on v1.2...

"I have to say your software is excellent. I have so much trouble playing a region 4 PAL disc "Screamers" on regular Powerdvd or Windvd. It skips frames especially in Chapter 17. But it seems that your software plays PAL discs and Pal VCD so flawlessly."

Larry Davis says regarding using v1.2 with laserdisc material...

"I installed the Win-TV yesterday along with the dTV software. I connected my laserdisc player to the Win-TV, launched dTV and I was stunned by how good the picture looked. It doesn't look as good as PC DVD, but it is a quantum leap beyond s-video input on the Asus geforce."

Li On compliments the initial release...

"I used S-video from a Pioneer LD player. Picture is too dark but adjusted the dTV video setting gave a great vivid picture. I tried StarWar TPM LD... and it gave a perfect picture with zero artifact! AMAZING! The picture stay in such perfect 'film mode'... I ended up watch the whole movie! Even the super fast motion in 'Pod Race' scene is perfect. I must said the picture quality is as great as my Faroudja processor!"
